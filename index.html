Borana Lodge is the most welcoming place you can tour in Kenya. The lodge is located in the laikipia plans with a good view of Mount Kenya

Best Activies

Borana lodge has the following as the main tourist activities.

  1. Swimming
  2. Game drive
  3. Horse riding
  4. Sundowner

The Big Five

Borana lodge has a number of the big five located in its surrounding borana conservancy.

The big five arround the lodge include the following

Lions
The lodge is surrounded by a pride of lions.
Elephants
The lodge has a herd of elephants present.
Buffola
The lodge has a herd of buffalo arround the area.
Rhino
There are also many Rhino in its sorrounding
Girraffe
The lodge has presence of many girraffes.

Location

Borana Lodge is located on the Laikipia plains north of MK.
